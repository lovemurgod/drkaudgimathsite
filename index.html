Our Legacy of Trusted Care

Founder: Dr. G. B. Karudgimath

Dr. G. B. Karudgimath built this hospital with a clear mission—to provide dependable, ethical, and patient-first medical care for Badami and nearby communities. His vision of structured treatment and human-centered care continues to define every service.

Continuing the Vision: Dr. Rajashekar G. Karudgimath

The second generation of leadership, Dr. Rajashekar G. Karudgimath, advances the hospital with modern practices while preserving the same core values of trust, safety, and community-focused care.
